<p><p>The ceramic tile cutter market features two primary types: wet tile cutters and dry tile cutters. Wet tile cutters utilize water to cool the blade and minimize dust, making them ideal for larger, thicker tiles and promoting a cleaner cutting process. Dry tile cutters, on the other hand, operate without water, relying on a durable blade to cut through tiles, which makes them more portable and easier to use in tight spaces. Each type serves distinct user needs based on project requirements.</p></p>
