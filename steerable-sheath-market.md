<p><strong>The Steerable Sheath Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Steerable Sheath</li><li>Bidirectional Steerable Sheath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steerable sheath market includes two primary types: unidirectional and bidirectional steerable sheaths. Unidirectional steerable sheaths allow for navigation in a single direction, providing precise control in procedures such as catheter insertion, enhancing maneuverability in confined spaces. In contrast, bidirectional steerable sheaths enable navigation in two directions, offering greater flexibility and improved access to complex anatomical areas. Both types are critical in minimally invasive surgeries, allowing clinicians to achieve enhanced dexterity and patient outcomes with reduced procedural trauma.</p></p>
