<p><ul><li>Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler</li><li>Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC)</li><li>Multi fuel firing boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel flexible boiler market encompasses various technologies tailored for efficient combustion of diverse fuels. The Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler excels in burning low-quality fuels, allowing for high efficiency and reduced emissions. The Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC) boiler is designed for lower pressures, accommodating a variety of fuels while maintaining operational flexibility. Multi fuel firing boilers enhance versatility by enabling the use of multiple fuels simultaneously, catering to varying energy needs and improving overall resource utilization.</p></p>
