<p><strong>The Cognitive Screening Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mini-Mental State Exam (MMSE)</li><li>Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA)</li><li>Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cognitive screening tools are vital for diagnosing and monitoring cognitive impairments. The Mini-Mental State Exam (MMSE) assesses cognitive function through a series of tasks, emphasizing memory and orientation. The Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA) offers a broader range of cognitive evaluation, focusing on memory, attention, language, and executive function. The Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR) assesses the severity and progression of dementia through observational analysis of cognitive and functional abilities. Together, these tools support early detection and management of cognitive disorders.</p></p>
