<p><ul><li>Solid Inkjet</li><li>Liquid inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet printers are classified into two main market types: solid inkjet and liquid inkjet. Solid inkjet uses solid ink sticks that are melted and applied to the paper, producing vibrant colors and reducing waste. This technology is often favored for high-volume printing in commercial environments. Conversely, liquid inkjet employs liquid ink cartridges that spray tiny droplets onto paper, offering versatility and high-quality output for both home and office use. Each type serves distinct imaging needs and user preferences.</p></p>
