Medium resistivity rings strike a balance, making them suitable for various electronic components. High resistivity silicon rings are ideal for applications in photonics, sensors, and high-frequency devices, where reduced leakage currents and improved performance are critical. Each type caters to specific industry needs, ensuring optimal functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
