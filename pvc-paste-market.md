<p><strong>The PVC Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC paste market consists of different production methods, primarily the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves suspending fine PVC particles in a liquid medium to create a stable paste, offering uniform properties and high stability. In contrast, the Emulsion Method uses an emulsifying agent to blend PVC with water, leading to a thicker paste suitable for various applications. Both methods cater to specific industry needs, influencing product characteristics and end-use applications.</p></p>
